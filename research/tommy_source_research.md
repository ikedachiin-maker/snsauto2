## Topic 1: ローンチマーケティング / Launch Marketing / "Cローンチ"

### Primary English Source: Jeff Walker - Product Launch Formula (PLF)
- **URL**: https://jeffwalker.com/programs/product-launch-formula/
- **Summary**: The original "Product Launch Formula" created in 2005. Teaches the "Sideways Sales Letter" - breaking a long sales letter into a sequence of pre-launch content delivered over 6-10 days. Students have generated over $1 billion in launches.
- **Key concept**: Pre-launch Content (PLC) sequence: Opportunity -> Transformation -> Ownership Experience
- **Tommy's adaptation**: His "Cローンチ" appears to be a variation - he describes it as "情弱狩り専用で教育なし" (targeting uninformed buyers, no education phase) but "なぜか売れる" (it still sells)
- Source: https://jeffwalker.com/product-launch-process/

### Jeff Walker's Sideways Sales Letter Framework
- **URL**: https://www.breakthroughmarketingsecrets.com/blog/jeff-walkers-4-part-sideways-sales-letter-framework-from-his-new-launch-book-product-launch-formula/
- **URL**: https://productlaunchstrategy.org/heres-how-the-sideways-sales-letter-powers-product-launch-formula/
- **URL**: https://systeme.io/blog/product-launch-summary
- **Summary**: Instead of one long sales letter, break it into 3 pieces of Pre-launch Content delivered over days. Uses Problem-Solution-Problem (PSP) framework.

### Frank Kern - Mass Control
- **URL**: https://www.ericstips.com/tips/mass-control/
- **URL**: https://frankkern.com/products/
- **Summary**: Frank Kern's 2008 launch generated $23.8M in less than 24 hours. His sales page was revolutionary - just a video, a few lines of text, and a buy button. "The art of selling without selling."
- **Tommy connection**: Tommy's approach of minimal/no education + simple sales page mirrors Kern's Mass Control philosophy.

### Japanese Source: プロダクトローンチ解説
- **URL**: https://seminars.jp/media/291
- **URL**: https://info-tokusoubu.com/product-launch/
- **URL**: https://the-lead-biz.com/product-launch/
- **Summary**: Japanese explainers of product launch methodology. The 4-step process: プレローンチ→リレーションシップ→ローンチ→ポストローンチ

### 平秀信 & Mr.X - Japan's Launch Origins

- **Summary**: The original Japanese product launch was done by Mr.X (ghostwriter) and 平秀信 in 2004-2005 with MMS (Master Marketing System), generating 165M yen in 24 hours.
